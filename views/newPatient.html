Frequently Asked Questions

Where is the office located?
Sunrise Acupuncture is located in the Executive Suite 5914, on the 5th floor of 400 Trade Center (the 400 building of TradeCenter 128).

Is there parking?
Yes, there is abundant garage and surface parking available. Parking is free.

Do acupuncture needles hurt?
Usually not. Acupuncture needles are hair-thin and insertion techniques make it painless. The acupuncturist will work with you to make sure that your treatment is comfortable.

Are the needles sterile?
Yes. They are disposed of after one use.

How should I prepare for a treatment?
It is a good idea to have something to eat and drink an hour before your treatment. Bring or wear comfortable and loose fitting clothing so the area being treated is accessible.

What will I experience during a treatment?
Some people don’t feel anything. Others feel sensations of tingling, warmth, heaviness, or a movement of energy along the meridian a point is on. These sensations indicate that qi is moving and the body is healing.

How many treatments will I need?
Treatment frequency and number of treatments needed depend on a variety of factors including the severity and duration of the condition, your constitution, your diet and lifestyle.
